# Seldon — Vision

## One-liner
Systematic knowledge preservation across discontinuities.

## Problem Statement

AI-assisted research writing fragments across conversation threads, context windows, and tool boundaries. Each session starts with partial knowledge of prior work. Citations drift. Structural coherence degrades. The gap between "I know things" and "I published a paper" grows with every context switch.

## Vision

A system that:
1. Maintains research state across session boundaries
2. Enforces citation discipline (APA 7th) through templates and tooling
3. Connects knowledge retrieval (Wintermute) with structural tracking (ANTS)
4. Produces publishable scholarly output from accumulated research sessions

## Key Insight

The problem isn't intelligence — LLMs are smart enough. The problem is continuity. Seldon solves continuity for research writing the way ANTS solves it for software engineering.

## Scope Boundaries

**Seldon IS:**
- Templates and conventions for research workflows
- Citation management and bibliography tooling
- Session continuity protocols (handoffs, briefings)
- Report assembly from structured components

**Seldon IS NOT:**
- A knowledge store (that's Wintermute)
- A traceability engine (that's ANTS)
- A replacement for a reference manager (use Zotero, etc.)
- A document editor

## Name Origin

Hari Seldon — Asimov's Foundation series. Built a system to preserve knowledge across a dark age of fragmentation. The Foundation was the organized output: the Encyclopedia Galactica.
